Quick Info

Harry Potter

The young wizard known as “The Boy Who Lived.”

Universe:
Harry Potter

Role:
Wizard

House:
Gryffindor

First Appearance:
Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

Backstory

Harry Potter is a young wizard who discovers on his eleventh birthday that he belongs to the wizarding world. He attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he learns magic, makes close friends, and faces many challenges connected to Lord Voldemort.

Abilities

Harry is skilled in Defense Against the Dark Arts and is known for his courage, quick thinking, and ability to cast a powerful Patronus Charm. He is also a talented Quidditch player and a strong leader when protecting his friends.

Personality

Harry is brave, loyal, and determined. He often puts others before himself and stands up for what is right, even when the situation is dangerous.
